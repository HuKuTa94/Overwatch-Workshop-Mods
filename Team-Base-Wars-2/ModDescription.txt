New gameplay experience like the RUST, Nuclear Dawn games. 
Each team has a commander who can build structures and use 
the commander's abilities. To win the game your team must reach $5000 first. 
Hold crouch + interaction ( C + F by default) to become a commander. 
Hold INTERACTION (F) to open shop and begin to build a base. 
To get money: 
- kill enemies
- steal from the enemy base
- capture resource point
- bring your loot to your base. 

Have fun!
